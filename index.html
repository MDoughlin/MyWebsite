Welcome to my World

Thanks for coming to my page! My name is Micheline and I was born and raised in Boston but I am a West Indian through and through. In 2019, I took a leap of faith and bought a one way ticket to Barbados and spent 10 months living with my grandmother on a farm. The best way to describe those months were intereting but I wouldn't change a thing that happened. Even though you will always find me in Barbados I am also of Jamaican heritage. I have always wanted a website or to put myself out there but it gives me serious anxiety but let's let this be the first step.

Fitness 🏋🏿

For the last 5 years fitness has played a huge role in my life. When I started it was just something to do then I set a goal to look like Bria Myles, which happened, but I slowly became my own goals and started to really challenge myself and it has paid off. Because I love fitness so much I became a Certified Personal Trainer and now help countless women transform into their best self
